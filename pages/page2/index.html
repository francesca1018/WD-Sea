FIRST PLAYABLE MELUSINE CHARACTER

Melusines are an all-female species in Fontaine. They are the "daughters" of Elynas. Sigewinne is the resident head nurse of the Fortress of Meropide. In the past, Sigewinne chose to adopt a human appearance in order to save a young child she befriended at a time where Melusines were looked down upon. However, her transformation was against Fontaine laws, resulting in her conviction to the Fortress of Meropide.
